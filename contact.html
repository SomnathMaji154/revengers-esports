Frequently Asked Questions

How can I support Things?

The best way to support us is to use our products, share them with friends, and provide feedback. We're also always looking for talented individuals to join our team!

How do I delete my account on Rooms?

You can delete your account from your profile settings in the Rooms app. This will permanently remove all your data and cannot be undone.

How does Things make money?

We offer both free and premium versions of our products. Premium features provide additional functionality for power users and teams.

What's the catch?

There's no catch! We believe in building great products that people love to use. Our business model is straightforward and transparent.

When will Rooms be on Android?

We'd like to bring Rooms to Android but do not yet have a target date. We're focused on making the iOS version as great as possible first.
